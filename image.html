1Mage Software Inc.

Pulsar Systems is pleased to represent 1MAGE, a line of document imaging and workflow solutions that reduce your cost of doing business.

Unwieldy paper trails and overflowing filing cabinets increase your costs of doing business, compromise security and thwart your disaster recovery efforts. Let 1mage Software's Document Imaging Solutions help you streamline those paper-intensive processes, while supporting your business compliance and recovery plans.

Introducing 1Magewf

1MAGEwf is a business process driven document management solution that solves business workflow problems by creating a more efficient flow of documents and information within your organization. Business process management (BPM) can be enhanced by automating and executing defined workflow tasks. 1MAGEwf combines open source technologies to achieve a cost effective approach to your work flow needs.

With 1mage Software, you can:

Capture Documents Quickly file, index, and archive all of your incoming and outgoing information whether paper or electronic; from emails and on-line forms to faxes.
Control Processes Efficiently retrieve, route, track, and manage information throughout your business processes for improved regulatory compliance
Share Information Securely share your vital business information among employees, business partners, and your customers.

How?

You can move your business processes from a system of complex paper trails and lost documents to 1mage Document Management and Retrieval System without missing a beat. Your customized Image Management Plan is specifically designed to help you integrate our imaging solutions with your existing business applications and infrastructure. From technology to scalability to deployment, the choice is yours

Further, with "Professional Services" your transition can be quick and painless with initial and ongoing training and support.

Your Choice

The 1mage complete line of document imaging software gives you a choice of Microsoft, Unix, or Linux platforms and a choice of networking solutions (LAN, WAN or Web). There is no need to start over because our system adapts to your technology and processes so you don't have to adopt to ours.

The following PDF data sheets are available for more information.

  • 1MAGE: The flagship document imaging/management system. File, route, track, archive and manage all incoming and outgoing information.
  • 1APIS: The Application Programming Interface suite allows simplified integration with your line-of-business application.
  • 1COLD: Get simultaneous, multi-user access to indexed and archived computer-generated reports
  • 1FAX: Efficiently manage your inbound and outbound faxes
  • 1FILE: An introductory, yet fully upgradable document imaging solution for up to ten users

Requires the addition of an Optical Character Recognition (OCR) engine to the installation
  • 1SUITE: With this series of Windows-based applications you can index, retrieve, view, print, e-mail, fax and more.
