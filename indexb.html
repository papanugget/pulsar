Pulsar Systems is a hardware/software consulting and internet security/technology firm that provides a wide range of services to companies seeking to start or improve their Internet utiliztion. We provide consulting and technology services including firewalls, VPN's, server hardware, and turnkey implementation. Our goal is to incorporate good design, strength, usability and creativity into solutions that will ultimately contribute to our client's success.

Pulsar Systems was founded in May of 1982 to provide software and services in the data processing industry. Originally the company specialized in the use of Prime Computers, later adding the IBM RS6000 (now System p), and then other UNIX based systems. Our focus is on UNIX and PC hardware, software and networking, the internet and security. We specialize in IBM p series systems.
